CONTRIBUTING TO LUMINA

Welcome. If you are reading this, it means you believe in the same thing we do.
That knowledge belongs to everyone. That no human being should be left without answers.
That what happened to Alexandria must never happen again.

Thank you for being here.


Who Can Contribute?

Everyone. You do not need to be a developer. You do not need a degree.
You just need to care.

👨‍💻 Developers
Help us build and improve the application.
Every line of code you write reaches someone in a forest, on a mountain, in the middle of the ocean.

🌐 Translators
Help us bring Lumina to every language on earth.
Including local and regional languages that the world has forgotten.

📚 Educators and Curators
Help us make sure the knowledge inside Lumina is accurate, clear, and accessible to everyone.

💛 Believers
Share Lumina with the world. Tell people why it exists.
Sometimes the most powerful contribution is simply spreading the word.


Our Standards

Every contributor is expected to follow these values.

Be honest. Only contribute knowledge that is accurate and can be verified.
Be neutral. No political agenda. No religious propaganda. Facts only.
Be kind. We are building something for all of humanity. Treat everyone with respect.
Be patient. This is a long journey. We are in it together.


How to Get Started

If you are a developer, check our open issues on GitHub and pick one to work on.
If you are a translator, reach out to us and we will guide you.
If you are an educator, start by reviewing existing content and suggesting improvements.
If you just want to help, send us a message. We will find a place for you.
